When it’s still a Charmander, it’s not the most reliable, but when it evolves into Charizard,
it can use its powerful moves to attack and deal damage over a wide area.

Charizard was first introduced in Generation 1.

Charizard is a Melee All-Rounder that deals Physical Attack damage.

Charizard's passive ability is Blaze, which increases critical-hit rate when at half HP or less.
