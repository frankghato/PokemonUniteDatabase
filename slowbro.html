Slowbro uses moves that freeze its opponents in ice, making them unable to move or attack.

Slowbro was first introduced in Generation 1.

Slowbro is a Ranged Defender that deals Special Attack damage.

Slowbro's passive ability is Oblivious, which causes HP loss to be gardual. When damaging an opposing Pokémon with a move,
the Pokémon nullifies some of this pending damage before it takes effect, and it also decreases the opposing Pokémon's Sp. Def
for a short time.
