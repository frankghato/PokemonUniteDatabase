Machamp is a Pokémon that specializes in melee combat, powered by its strong fighting moves.

Machamp was first introduced in Generation 1.

Machamp is a Melee All-Rounder that deals Physical Attack damage.

Machamp's passive ability is Guts, which increases Attack when afflicted by a status condition.
