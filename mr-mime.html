Mr. Mime specializes in hindering opposing Pokémon with its tricky moves.

Mr. Mime was first introduced in Generation 1.

Mr. Mime is a Melee Supporter that deals Special Attack damage.

Mr. Mime's passive ability is Filter, which reduces damage received for a short time after it uses a move.
