Crustle hinders its opponents using many different moves.

Crustle was first introduced in Generation 5.

Crustle is a Melee Defender that deals Physical Attack damage.

Crustle's passive ability is Sturdy, which increases Defense and Sp. Defense after losing a certain amount of HP.
