Garchomp’s Ability, Rough Skin, means it can sometimes cause damage to Pokémon that hit it, so
opponents that attack Garchomp carelessly may just get struck back. What’s more, using Dig lets
Garchomp sneak-attack its opponents from underground.

Garchomp was first introduced in Generation 4.

Garchomp is a Melee All-Rounder that deals Physical Attack damage.

Garchomp's passive ability is Rough Skin, which reflects damage back to the opponent when struck with a melee attack.
